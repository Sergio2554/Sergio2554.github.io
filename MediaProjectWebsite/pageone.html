Memphis Pyramid

The Memphis Pyramid is now a Bass Pro Shop with many attractions to offer!

Highlights

- A vast shopping center awaits for all your fishing and camping needs!

- The top of the pyramid is reachable through a 28-story elevator ride that will take you to a breathtaking view of downtown Memphis and the Mississippi River!

- A delicious dining experience in The Lookout sits at the peak of the city!

- Turn in for the night at the Big Cypress Lodge! A place with rustic charm with rooms such as those overlooking the Bass Pro shop and more.
