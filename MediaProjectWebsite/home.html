Tourism in Memphis

Tourism is a big industry that can support cities like ours with money from tourists who come to visit.
In 2023, over 144 million visitors came to our state, 13.5 million of those coming to our city.
This in turn helped our city by creating jobs and bringing revenue to our local economy.

Tourism can bring important things to the table for our city such as employment opportunities and money.
Apart from that however, to attract tourists in the first place the city would have to create experiences and attractions in turn.
This website was made for those who don't see how tourism can change one's city for the better.
This is also for those who might not have seen first hand the places that visitors and citizens alike can enjoy
from a city that grows their tourism sector and the landmarks that makes one's place home.
The links shown are only a few of the places that one can experience ranging from food and drinks to attractions and entertainment
for those who want places to enjoy in our city.
